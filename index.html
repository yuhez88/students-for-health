Knowledge Is Care.

Students for Health is a student-run organization that aims to make health information easier to find and discuss. We connect our community with reliable health information and local resources, while fostering a culture of support, care, and awareness through student-driven outreach.

Monthly Health Tips

Each month, we create concise, easy-to-understand flyers on health topics such as reproductive health, mental wellness, and saving money on prescriptions. These flyers are distributed across campuses, libraries, community centers like the YMCA, food pantries, and other places where community members gather.

Past topics include “Planned Parenthood: What Do They Actually Do?” and “Save Money with GoodRx.”

Monthly Events

Once a month, we host open discussions on health and wellness topics through Google Meet. Everyone is welcome to join, and participation can be completely anonymous—cameras and microphones are entirely optional!

You’re invited to ask questions, share experiences, or simply listen in. We’re here to help you connect with resources, find answers, and be by your side.
