Monthly Event

Each month, we host an online chat on Google Meet about a health and wellness topic. These chats are casual and open to everyone, just join, listen, and share if you want to. No camera or microphone is required, and you can join under a nickname if you'd like to.

We created these chats to make it easier for people to ask questions, learn from other, and talk about things that matter.
